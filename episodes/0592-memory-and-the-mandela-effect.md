Ben is sick of hearing about The Mandela Effect.

# Mandela Effect Examples
- Nelson Mandela died in prison
- Berenstein Bears
- "Luke, I am your father"
- C3PO was all gold
- The Monopoly man had a monocle
- My baloney had a second name, and it was M-E-Y-E-R
- Sinbad starred as a genie in a movie called Shazam
- JIFFY brand peanut butter
- Mr. Roger's sang "It's a beautiful day in the neighborhood"
- Billy Graham died
- In Disney Snow White, the queen says, "Mirror mirror on the wall, who is the fairest of them all?"

Parallel universes? A glitch in the matrix? Time travel?

Matt points out that there was a Snow White movie titled "Mirror Mirror" even though the Disney movie line was, "Magic mirror on the wall..." {% arc %}Disney did not originate Snow White. Snow White is a German folk tale (although there are versions from other cultures). There is no "original" version of the story in German. Even if there were, there would be no "one true" translation in English with "the correct" wording of the line. Jack Zipes, folklorist (and professor emeritus at the University of Minnesota) used the phrase, "Mirror, mirror, on the wall" in his 1987 translation.{% endarc %}
